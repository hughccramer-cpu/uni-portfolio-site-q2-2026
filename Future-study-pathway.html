Future study pathway

My study direction

Industrial design, I currently would like to continue to study in the industrial design stream, but I know that if I change my mind I will not be stuck on this pathway. This year I have worked on many industrial design or adjacent uni and personal projects, and have found them to be both fun and challenging but especially rewarding. I have found working on physical projects to be very fun and to be a way of learning that I take well.
Recently I have been enjoying learning about and using electronic instruments, studying industrial design will let me build and experiment as I learn more about this topic. I also have pre-existing skills in cg art and 3d visualisation which will be relevant for industrial design.

Unit selection

Three units I am looking forward to doing are:

3D Digital Design Fundamentals, Which will help me improve my CAD (computer aided design) skills and my ability to design for manufacturability. I hope that I will be able to make better work faster and CAD changes from a barrier to a tool in my projects.

Design Culture and Society, I have very little understanding of design history, trends and culture. I aim to amend this during this unit and create a stronger foundation for me to build novel and impactful work.

Front-end Web Design, Last year I took intro to interaction design and quite enjoyed it and gained many skills during it that I have made good use of, see this website. I also believe that my ability to create an interesting website will be important in my future career as I will need to have a portfolio.

Two of these units (3D Digital Design Fundamentals and design culture and society) are part of the industrial design stream and (Front-end Web Design) is part of interaction design, doing these further my current goals and if I later choose to study interaction design I already have one unit done.
