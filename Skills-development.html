Research

So far in uni I have learned a new approach to doing research than what I had used in high school, focusing on looking at smaller events that are more relevant to my work is more valuable and can offer more valuable insights compared to events that have been heavily researched. As well as looking at case studies to learn directly from other successes and failures.

In assignment 1 I mentioned that I wanted to get better at it. I think I am. I have learned how to use Affinity Layout (an InDesign-like software) and have made this document as a website.
